CHANGES IN THE BODY

  • As the baby grows, you will begin to show and may experience mood swings, intense food cravings, fatigue, weight gain, morning sickness, tender breasts, and nausea.
  • During this week, many women have their first ultrasound done to make sure that everything is alright with mother and baby and to get a first glimpse of their embryo.

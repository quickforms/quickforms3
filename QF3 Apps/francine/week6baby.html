YOUR BABY'S DEVELOPMENT

    Size of baby: pea (2-4 mm)
  • Weight: 0.01 ounces
  • Length: 0.30 inch (crown rump length)
  • Your baby now has distinguishable body and head, and is starting to develop facial features. Your baby's brain and cranial nerves are forming. At this stage, the neural tube closes in your baby's back. Baby's heart continue to grow and now beats at a regular rhythm (normal heart rate of baby- 100-140 beats per minute).
  • The lungs, kidneys, liver, intestines, and pancreas are all forming. The baby's arm and leg buds have formed webbed structures that will become hands and feet.
  • Your baby's heartbeat may be visible through a vaginal ultrasound. His/ her eyes and nostrils will appear on the ultrasound as black dots, and limbs that look like stubs protrude from the body.
